Self-Charging Shoe: A Step Towards Sustainable Energy

Problem Statement Proposed Solution Technology Expected Outcomes Challenges

Problem Statement

Traditional electronic devices rely on batteries that need frequent charging, limiting their usability. Battery disposal also poses significant environmental concerns due to toxic materials.

Proposed Solution: Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting

We propose a self-charging shoe concept that uses piezoelectric technology to harness energy from human movement by embedding piezoelectric crystals in the shoe sole.

Technology and How it Works

Piezoelectric Effect

Piezoelectric materials generate electric charge when subjected to mechanical stress. Walking causes piezoelectric crystals to deform, creating a voltage difference.

Energy Harvesting and Storage Process

  1. Voltage Generation: Piezoelectric crystals generate AC voltage
  2. Rectification: AC voltage converted to DC voltage
  3. Voltage Boosting: Optional circuit to increase voltage
  4. Energy Storage: DC voltage stored in capacitor or battery

Potential Device Powering

  • Wearable Electronics
  • Mobile Phones
  • Environmental Sensors

Expected Outcomes

  • Self-sufficient devices requiring minimal external power
  • Reduced environmental impact
  • Sustainable energy solution
  • Innovative applications in remote areas
